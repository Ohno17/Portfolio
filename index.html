Hello, my name is Ajay.

I first got into programming when I started making simple games for my friends in middle school, like text-based adventures and small 2D platformers using visual scripting tools like Scratch. I liked turning my ideas into these interactive experiences, and seeing people play what I made kept me motivated.

What started as a way to share something fun became a deeper interest in software. Along the way, I found that robotics is interesting to me because it showed how my code could immediately have an effect on the world. Both VEX and FIRST Robotics programs were a valuable learning experience, helping me build engineering and collaboration skills. I spent time learning languages and tools, not just to share fun, but to code for the fun of it.
