Computer Science Portfolio

Hello, my name is Ajay.

I first started to code when I was 9 years old because I wanted to make games for my friends to play. I started on Scratch, learning the basics of programming. After making a couple games with Scratch, I realized that I wanted to be a programmer. When I had grown tired of Scratch's limitations, I started making games on Roblox, where I utilized a programming language named Lua. Now that I had learned Lua, a text-based language, I started to branch out into other languages like Python, Java, C, and HTML/CSS. I took classes at Code Ninja for a year and learned Javascript. From there I continued to code and make new projects in different programming languages. With my skills in programming, I taught the people around me how to code.

In our middle school's VEX Robotics club, I was the lead programmer in our team, who had gotten 4th place. I had learned to take into account the design of the robot as well as the driver's feedback in order to create an effective program.

When I was participating in a First Robotics team, teamwork was an important part of building our robot. We worked together, and discussed solutions, developing important engineering skills in the process. I had learned how to gather and evaluate information to make decisions, and because of that, I felt that my experience on the team was very rewarding and educational.
